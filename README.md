# MBBS in Kyrgyzstan – Complete Guide for Indian Students

Kyrgyzstan has become one of the most popular destinations for Indian students who want to study MBBS abroad at an affordable cost. Every year thousands of students choose Kyrgyzstan because of low fees, NMC approved universities and English medium education.

👉 Official Website: https://www.mymbbsadmission.com/mbbs-abroad/mbbs-in-kyrgyzstan

---

## 🎓 Why Study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan?

• Affordable total fees (15–25 Lakhs)  
• NMC & WHO approved universities  
• English medium MBBS course  
• No donation / No entrance exam  
• Safe country for Indian students  
• High FMGE passing ratio  

---

## 🏥 MBBS Course Duration

| Course | Duration |
|--------|-----------|
| MBBS in Kyrgyzstan | 5 Years Study + 1 Year Internship |

---

## 💰 MBBS Fees in Kyrgyzstan

Average tuition fees: **$3,500 – $5,000 per year**

Total package (including hostel):  
👉 **15–25 Lakhs INR**

Much cheaper than Indian private medical colleges.

---

## 🏫 Top Medical Universities in Kyrgyzstan

• Kyrgyz State Medical Academy  
• Osh State University  
• Jalal-Abad State University  
• International School of Medicine  

---

## 📋 Eligibility Criteria

• 50% in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)  
• NEET qualified  
• Minimum age 17 years  

---

## ✈️ Admission Process (Step-by-Step)

1️⃣ Apply online  
2️⃣ Receive admission letter  
3️⃣ Pay registration fees  
4️⃣ Apply for visa  
5️⃣ Fly to Kyrgyzstan 🎉  

---

## 🏠 Hostel & Food

• Separate hostel for boys & girls  
• Indian mess available  
• Fully safe & secure campuses  

---

## 🌍 Career After MBBS

After completing MBBS from Kyrgyzstan students can:

• Appear for FMGE/NEXT (India)  
• Apply for USMLE (USA)  
• Apply for PLAB (UK)  
• Work worldwide 🌎
